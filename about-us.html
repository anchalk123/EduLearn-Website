We Bridge the Gap in Education

EduLearn was built with a simple goal: to make sure that no classroom is ever left unattended. We connect professional, verified substitute teachers with school districts seamlessly using smart technology.

By providing flexibility to substitute teachers and reliability to school administrations, we keep the student learning graph constantly growing and on track.

🎯 Our Mission

To provide schools with reliable, instant access to premium educators, ensuring classroom scheduling remains completely unaffected during regular teacher absences.

👁️ Our Vision

To establish a fully digitalized, highly active ecosystem where substitute teachers find flexible jobs, and education is never compromised anywhere.
